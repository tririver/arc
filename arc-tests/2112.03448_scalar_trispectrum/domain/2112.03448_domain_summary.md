# Cosmological collider and cosmological bootstrap for scalar trispectra

This domain studies primordial cosmological correlators, especially scalar trispectra, using bootstrap, symmetry, unitarity, cutting, dispersion, and related amplitude-inspired methods. The main goal is to reconstruct late-time inflationary observables directly from boundary consistency conditions, often by relating them to flat-space amplitudes, then extending to exchange, spin, loop, and strongly coupled sectors.

## Foundation Paper

- Paper: arXiv:1811.00024 - The Cosmological Bootstrap: Inflationary Correlators from Symmetries and Singularities
- Reason: It directly develops analytic four-point inflationary correlators and the symmetry/singularity bootstrap logic used by later trispectrum and cosmological-collider papers.

## Methodology

- Core method: fix correlators from symmetries, singularities, and absence of unphysical poles rather than bulk time evolution.
  Papers: arXiv:1811.00024, arXiv:2103.08649, arXiv:2009.02898
- Weight-shifting and spin-raising operators generate spinning or massless external correlators from scalar seeds.
  Papers: arXiv:1811.00024, arXiv:2005.04234, arXiv:1910.14051
- Contact trispectra and higher-point contact terms can be reconstructed from flat-space amplitudes via contact reconstruction / dressing rules.
  Papers: arXiv:2106.15468, arXiv:2602.03841, arXiv:2404.05712
- Unitarity-based cutting rules and dispersion relations reconstruct correlators from discontinuities and enforce sum rules.
  Papers: arXiv:2104.06587, arXiv:2602.05546, arXiv:2602.03841
- Loop and spectral methods extend the program beyond tree level, including one-loop and resummed signals.
  Papers: arXiv:2211.03810, arXiv:2601.00952, arXiv:2602.09977, arXiv:2408.16386

## Open Questions

- How can exchange trispectra be reconstructed as cleanly as contact terms, especially beyond tree level?
  Papers: arXiv:2106.15468, arXiv:2602.05546, arXiv:2502.04434
- What is the systematic boundary-only formulation of the boostless bootstrap for exchange and spinning correlators?
  Papers: arXiv:2010.12818, arXiv:2205.00013, arXiv:2602.07117
- What is the general loop-level basis, and how do singularities organize across loops and different backgrounds?
  Papers: arXiv:2211.03810, arXiv:2601.00952, arXiv:2408.16386
- Can the geometric/combinatorial structures (cosmohedra, tubings, cluster adjacency, kinematic flow) unify full correlators, not just integrands or selected graph families?
  Papers: arXiv:2412.19881, arXiv:2504.14890, arXiv:2603.08670, arXiv:2312.05303
- How far do the cosmological collider signatures remain distinguishable once one includes realistic backgrounds, boosts breaking, loops, and observational systematics?
  Papers: arXiv:2603.15728, arXiv:2502.06931, arXiv:2602.12232, arXiv:2602.09977
